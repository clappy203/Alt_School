My Favorite Jollof rice with Chicken

This is a simple recipe for making Jollof rice with chicken.

Recipe Information:

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour
  • Servings: 3-4 scoops of Jellof rice with chicken and a bowl of salad
  • Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of Jellof rice
  • 1 kg of chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, blended
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • 4-5 cubes of seasoning

Instructions:

  1. Prepare all ingredients before starting the cooking process.
  2. Start by blending the tomatoes and fresh peppers until smooth.
  3. Chop the onions, carrots, garlic and other vegetables finely.
  4. Then wash the chicken pieces thoroughly under cold water.
  5. Set a pot with water to boil for the chicken.
  6. Once the water is getting warm, add the chicken pieces and certain spices like one teaspoon of salt, pepper, tomatoes (a small portion from the one blended from earlier), thyme and 2-cubes of seasoning.
  7. Let the chicken boil for about 25-30 minutes, then remove it from the pot and set aside.
  8. Rinse the rice in cold water and set aside.
  9. Put a pot of water on to boil for the rice.
  10. Then put the rinsed rice into the boiling water.
  11. Cook the rice for about 20 minutes or until tender.
  12. Drain the rice and set aside.
  13. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  14. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until translucent.
  15. Add the blended tomatoes, thyme, curry powder, salt, and pepper. Cook for 10 minutes.
  16. Add the chicken pieces and brown on all sides.
  17. Add the chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  18. Add the rinsed rice, stir well, and reduce the heat to low.
  19. Cover the pot and simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  20. Serve hot with a side of salad.

Tips:

  • For extra flavor, marinate the chicken pieces in spices and a bit of oil for a few hours before cooking.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables like bell peppers or peas to the rice for added nutrition.
  • Serve with a side of fried plantains for a complete meal.
